PYQUEN event generator

Purpose

PYQUEN is event generator for simulation of rescattering, radiative and collisional energy loss of hard partons in expanding quark-gluon plasma created in ultrarelativistic heavy ion AA collisions (implemented as modification of standard pythia6.4xx jet event)

Method

I.P. Lokhtin, A.M. Snigirev, ``Nuclear geometry of jet quenching'', Eur. Phys. J C16 (2000) 527-536

I.P.Lokhtin, A.M.Snigirev, `` Fast simulation jet quenching in ultrarelativistic heavy ion collisions'', Preprint SINP MSU 2004-13/752, hep-ph/0406038

I.P. Lokhtin, A.M. Snigirev, ``A model of jet quenching in ultrarelativistic heavy ion collisions and high-pt hadron spectra at RHIC'', Eur. Phys. J C46 (2006) 211-217
